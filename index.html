Payment Policy

10% on Confirmation of the Order: When a customer places an order with Kraft India, they are required to pay 10% of the total order amount upfront at the time of confirming the order. This payment serves as a commitment from the customer to proceed with the purchase.

Rest on Dispatch Time: The remaining 90% of the payment is to be made at the time of dispatch. This means that Kraft India will prepare the order and get it ready for shipment, and once it's ready to be sent to the customer, the customer must pay the outstanding balance before the goods are actually dispatched or delivered to them.

Bulk Orders (50% Advance): In the case of bulk orders, which typically involve larger quantities or higher values, Kraft India requires a 50% advance payment. This means that for bulk orders, the customer needs to pay half of the total order amount upfront when confirming the order. The remaining 50% is due at the time of dispatch, similar to standard orders.
